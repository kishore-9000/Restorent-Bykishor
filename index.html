The Story of Kishore Cafe

Founded on the principles of authenticity and hospitality, Kishore Cafe has been serving the community with recipes passed down through generations. Our spices are ground fresh every day, and our ingredients are sourced locally to ensure every bite is a journey through India.

Whether you're looking for a quick street-food snack or a celebratory feast, we welcome you to join our family at the table.
